Project Title:
EcoTrack AI – Intelligent Carbon Footprint Tracker and Sustainability Planner

Project Domain:
Artificial Intelligence | Machine Learning | Sustainability | Green Skills | Data Analytics

Project Description:
EcoTrack AI is an AI-powered personal and organizational carbon footprint analyzer that processes user consumption habits (energy consumption, transit choices, food habits, and waste) to classify user profiles and recommend custom sustainability steps. Using K-Means clustering, the system groups users with similar consumption patterns and offers targeted recommendations on reducing footprints. It features interactive graphs of footprint breakdowns, a chatbot assistant providing quick environmental advice, and actionable daily/weekly sustainability challenges to build green skills.
